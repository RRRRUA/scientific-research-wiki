# HFRW

HFRW is a localized invisible image watermarking system. It uses a dueling DQN to select a patch for embedding, CBAM to improve the encoder and decoder, and localization/synchronization before message recovery.

Its stated objective is to retain ordinary-image fidelity and low file-size growth while preserving robustness to common transformations.
